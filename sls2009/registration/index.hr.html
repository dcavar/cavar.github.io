SLS članstvo, registracija i članarina

SLS pravila propisuju da izlagači moraju biti članovi SLS-a. Poveznica na informaciju o načinu učlanjenja može se naći na mrežnim stranicama SLS-a:

http://www.utexas.edu/world/sls/

Na stranicama se navodi: "The way to become a member of the Slavic Linguistic Society is to subscribe to its scholarly journal, the Journal of Slavic Linguistics. When you subscribe to the journal, you are automatically registered as a member of the Society for the period of your subscription. Information on rates and on how to do so is found here." Poveznica na obrazac za registraciju pretplate časopisa nalazi se tu:

http://www.slavica.com/journals/jsl/subscriptions.html
